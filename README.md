# E-fishery application
E-fishery application is the android application developed to bring the fishing business on digital platform. Through this application fisherman can sale fresh fishes available. Along with it they can also sale the devices and materials required for fishing such as oil, fishing net, ice etc. It can bring wholesale retailer and customer nearer. This application contains a very simple User-Interface which is very handy to use.

## Guide
This application mainly contains two parts: Fishery Society and Customer.

<img src="https://github.com/1405yuga/E-fishery-application/assets/82303711/fbd35df1-ef9b-49ad-baa8-0d2e60e781e1" width="250" height="500">

### Fishery Society:
Fishery Socitey is the business part handled by fishermen. Here they can update stocks available and price of fishes as well as fishing products.

### Customer:
Customer can view and place the order of desired product through this section.The sections included in Customer are: Fresh fishes, Oil, Engine/Spare parts, Fish Farming seeds, Ice factory and Contact.

It displays the Fresh fishes with quantity available and the price imposed on it.

Customer can place the order by clicking on cart icon. Order Panel will be opened where customer is needs to enter the desired quantity.

<img src="https://github.com/1405yuga/E-fishery-application/assets/82303711/49a01ef9-0e9b-4581-9c1a-b360890fd8a8" width="250" height="500">

Customer needs to fill all the details and press Order button to place the order. This Order will be updated in Fishery Society in real time.
