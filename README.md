# E-fishery application
E-fishery application is the android application developed to bring the fishing business on digital platform. Through this application fisherman can sale fresh fishes available. Along with it they can also sale the devices and materials required for fishing such as oil, fishing net, ice etc. It can bring wholesale retailer and customer nearer. This application contains a very simple User-Interface which is very handy to use.

## Guide
This application mainly contains two parts: Fishery Society and Customer.

### Fishery Society:

Fishery Socitey is the business part handled by fishermen. Here they can update stocks available and price of fishes as well as fishing products.
Fishery Sociter can be logged in by entering password in the dialog appeared. Here, passowrd is hardcoded.

<img src="https://github.com/1405yuga/E-fishery-application/assets/82303711/4851d28f-5f76-4398-bb3e-0382bc70e174" width="250" height="500">

Fishery society contains sections:Fresh fishes, Oil, Engine/Spare parts, Fish Farming seeds, Ice factory and Contact where they can update. Along with it list of Orders are displayed in order section.
